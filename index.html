Frequently Asked Questions

How many kilometers in a mile?
1 mile = 1.60934 kilometers. To convert miles to km, multiply by 1.60934. To convert km to miles, multiply by 0.62137.
How do you convert Fahrenheit to Celsius?
Subtract 32, then multiply by 5/9. Formula: C = (F - 32) x 5/9. For example, 98.6F = 37C (body temperature).
How many pounds in a kilogram?
1 kilogram = 2.20462 pounds. To convert kg to lbs, multiply by 2.20462. To convert lbs to kg, divide by 2.20462.
How many cups in a liter?
1 liter = 4.22675 US cups. 1 US cup = 236.588 milliliters. These conversions are commonly needed for cooking and baking recipes.
